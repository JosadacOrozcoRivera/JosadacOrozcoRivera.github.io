Quantum Engineering Student Club Board Member (2022-2023)

CETYS University, Tijuana, Mx.

Description

From August 2022 to September 2023, i served as a board member for the student engineering club "Quantum". As the Mechanical Design Lead, some of my responsibilities included:

  • Be the lead mechanical designer for the ARLISS project, 2022 & 2023.
  • Participate in engineering conferences and leadership events.
  • Collaborate in volunteering work for underprivileged communities, teaching use of Arduino and electronics.
  • Meet and interact with other engineering student clubs from around the world.
  • Assisting in the planning of conferences at campus such as a Women in Engineering talk by a Tesla Engineer.
