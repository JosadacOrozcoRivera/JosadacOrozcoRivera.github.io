Solar Turbines Engineering Internship (1yr)

CETYS University, Tijuana, Mx.

Description

From March 2024 to March 2025, I undertook an internship as a Hydromechanical Design Engineer at Solar Turbines, in the Package Refurbishment & Upgrade (PRU) department. During this period, I had the opportunity to contribute significantly to various projects focused on upgrading and retrofitting turbine hydromechanical subsystems . I developed Process and Instrumentation Diagrams (P&IDs) in accordance with ISA S5.1 standards, enhancing the clarity of system designs for components such as start-up, fuel, lubrication oil, seal, enclosure, and fire and gas systems.

Futhermore, I developed key interpersonal skills such as effective communication through technical presentations and training sessions. I demonstrated adaptability by working across various turbine subsystems, attention to detail in technical diagrams, and strong collaboration skills while interacting with field representatives, new team members, and customers.
